About Institute Of Life Science

The Institute of Life Sciences (ILS) is a premier research institute in India, dedicated to advancing knowledge in the field of life sciences. With a focus on cutting-edge research, the institute addresses critical areas like infectious diseases, cancer biology, structural biology, and plant and microbial biotechnology.
