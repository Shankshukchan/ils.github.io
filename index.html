Our Establishment

Institute of Life Science is an institute dedicated to provide practical based and job - oriented education. It was established in May 2018. It is running under a recognized society “Modern Education Society’” registered under Act 21, 1860 by UP Government.

Well Established Labs

Our institute takes pride in its well-established laboratory, equipped with an extensive range of modern lab equipment and a comprehensive selection of chemical facilities.

Smart Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with advanced technology, such as interactive digital boards, high-speed internet, and multimedia tools, creating an engaging environment for students.
