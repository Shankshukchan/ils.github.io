Our Establishment

Institute of Life Science is an institute dedicated to provide practical based and job - oriented education. It was established in May 2018. It is running under a recognized society “Modern Education Society’” registered under Act 21, 1860 by UP Government. We provide all UG, PG, Vocational and Paramedical courses through an UGC approved University “Asian International University”, Imphal, Manipur (For further enquiry visit university website).
